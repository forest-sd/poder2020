Register to Vote

How to Register

Registering to vote takes less than five minutes and can be done entirely online in most states. You'll need your state ID or driver's license number, the last four digits of your Social Security number, and your current address. If you've recently moved, changed your name, or aren't sure if you're registered, check your status and update your information.

Pledge to Vote
