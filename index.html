Empowering Latinx communities to register, vote, and shape the future of our democracy. The 2020 election marked the first time Latinx voters were the largest racial or ethnic group in the electorate.

Register to Vote

Our Mission

Poder 2020 strategically engages in voter registration through targeted media outreach to young Latinx adults ages 18-35. We produce videos in English and Spanish that motivate and inspire young Latinx voters to protect our community through civic participation. With an estimated 15 to 18 million eligible Latinx voters who have not yet registered, the potential for political participation is extraordinary.
